01 — About

A wide-angle view of how work gets done.

I'm an early-career technology professional who has spent the last few years collecting an unusually well-rounded view of how things actually get built and run. I've remediated MFA and identity issues for business clients, facilitated sprint stand-ups inside a platform-engineering team, coordinated a supermarket front-end during a Saturday rush, and pitched an AI proof-of-concept to mentors at IBM.

That range is the point. I'm genuinely fascinated by the space where technology meets the people who use it and the processes that surround it — where a cleaner ServiceNow workflow, a shared Confluence template, or a calmer stand-up quietly makes an entire team faster.

What pulls me in is work where technical understanding, business context, communication, and process all overlap: IT and systems, cloud and identity, solutions engineering, technical sales, and Agile delivery. I'm early in the journey, relentlessly curious, coachable, and I learn fastest by doing — which is exactly why I'm excited to be returning to TJX this fall.
